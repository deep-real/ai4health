About

AI4Health Industry day is back! Join us for a one-day summit focused on real-world AI in healthcare, from predictive modeling and diagnostics to personalized care and operational improvements. This year, we are zooming in on clinical applications and primary care, especially across the Delaware Valley and tristate region. Researchers, health systems, policymakers, and industry leaders will meet to share problems, prototype ideas, and build partnerships that last.

Jesse Tetreault is a Senior Solutions Architect at NVIDIA, specializing in accelerating biomedical research and healthcare applications through GPU computing and AI. Since joining NVIDIA in 2017, Jesse has supported academic medical centers, federal healthcare institutions, and enterprise customers on initiatives ranging from architecting GPU-accelerated data centers to collaborating on scientific publications focused on AI applications in radiology. He holds a B.S. and M.S. in Computer Engineering from Clemson University, where his research focused on deep learning for computer vision in autonomous driving.
