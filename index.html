About

This event will provide a unique platform for researchers to present cutting-edge developments in AI and health, spanning areas such as predictive modeling, diagnostic tools, personalized medicine, and medical imaging. By bringing together academia and industry, AI4Health aims to drive innovative solutions that address pressing challenges in healthcare, from improving patient outcomes to optimizing healthcare delivery systems.

This one-day workshop also serves as a launching point for research and educational partnerships, where students and faculty can engage directly with industry partners to bridge the gap between theoretical advancements and real-world applications. By facilitating cross-disciplinary dialogue, AI4Health aims to nurture collaborative projects, internships, and research initiatives that empower the next generation of AI researchers and practitioners. Through this workshop, we aim to establish a foundation for sustainable, impactful collaborations that will contribute to the future of AI in health/healthcare.
